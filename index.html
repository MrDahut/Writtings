Independent Thought

— a public workshop for human-centered research, writing, and design — scaffolded by AI, governed by rigor.

🧭 Mission

This site is a living case-study in the responsible use of artificial intelligence for augmenting human inquiry. Every word passes through research, interrogation, structural refinement, and conscious authorship before publication.

🔧 Our Method

  • Frame the Question — define unknowns and required evidence.
  • Interrogate the System — historical, legal, and technical probing with AI tools.
  • Draft the Skeleton — human-authored voice, pacing, logic.
  • Backfill with Support — AI surfaces gaps, typos, structural weak points.
  • Finalize and Publish — human editor retains responsibility; no hallucinations.

Full methodology: Thought Generation, Not Content Generation.
